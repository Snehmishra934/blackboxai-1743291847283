Shailputri

The daughter of the mountains, Shailputri is the first form of Goddess Durga worshipped on the first day of Navratri.

Significance:

Shailputri represents nature and purity. She is depicted riding a bull, carrying a trident and lotus in her hands.

Mantra:

ॐ देवी शैलपुत्र्यै नमः॥

Om Devi Shailaputryai Namah॥

Color of the Day:

Yellow - Symbolizing happiness and brightness
