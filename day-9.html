Siddhidatri

The granter of supernatural powers, she represents the culmination of spiritual journey.

Significance:

She bestows all types of Siddhis (supernatural powers). Depicted sitting on a lotus with four hands holding a mace, discus, lotus and book, surrounded by gods and devotees.

Mantra:

ॐ देवी सिद्धिदात्र्यै नमः॥

Om Devi Siddhidatryai Namah॥

Color of the Day:

Purple - Symbolizing spirituality and wisdom
