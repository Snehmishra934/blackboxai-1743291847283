Mahagauri

The radiant and peaceful form of Parvati, representing purity and serenity.

Significance:

She symbolizes beauty and grace. Depicted wearing white clothes, with four hands holding a trident and damaru while making blessing and protective gestures, riding a bull.

Mantra:

ॐ देवी महागौर्यै नमः॥

Om Devi Mahagauryai Namah॥

Color of the Day:

Pink - Symbolizing compassion and hope
