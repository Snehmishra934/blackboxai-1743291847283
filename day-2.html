Brahmacharini

The ascetic form of Goddess Parvati, Brahmacharini represents penance and devotion.

Significance:

She symbolizes the pursuit of wisdom and knowledge. Depicted walking barefoot, holding a rosary and water pot.

Mantra:

ॐ देवी ब्रह्मचारिण्यै नमः॥

Om Devi Brahmacharinyai Namah॥

Color of the Day:

Green - Symbolizing growth and harmony
